Our Services

Everything you need to source with confidence.

From finding the right manufacturer to delivering your product — we manage every step of international sourcing so you can focus on growing your business.

What We Offer

Six services. One seamless experience.

Each service works on its own or as part of a complete sourcing solution — tailored to your product, market, and goals.

🌐

Product Sourcing

We identify the right manufacturers for your product — matching quality, capacity, and price to your exact needs across our four-country network.

  • Manufacturer identification & shortlisting
  • Quotation management & comparison
  • Sample coordination & evaluation
  • Clear supplier recommendations
🤝

Supplier Development

We turn potential manufacturers into trusted, long-term partners through in-person audits and relationship building.

  • Factory audits & capability assessment
  • Certification & compliance verification
  • On-site visits & face-to-face meetings
  • Ongoing performance tracking
📊

Strategic Procurement

We negotiate complete value — pricing, terms, quality, and timelines — so every deal supports your long-term success.

  • Full landed-cost analysis
  • Price, MOQ & payment term negotiation
  • Quality specifications & inspections
  • Purchase order management
🏭

OEM & ODM Manufacturing

Whether you have a finished design or need a ready-made product to brand, we connect you with factories that deliver exactly what your market demands.

  • OEM vs. ODM strategy guidance
  • Factory matching & capability review
  • Sample development & revisions
  • Production oversight & quality control
🏷️

Private Label Development

Build your own brand on world-class manufacturing. We manage the full journey — from concept to branded, shelf-ready product.

  • Category research & factory selection
  • Branding & packaging coordination
  • Compliance review (FDA, FCC, CE)
  • Production & pre-shipment inspection
📦

Import & Supply Chain Support

We keep your goods moving smoothly — with clean documentation, clear costs, and resilient supply chain strategies across all four countries.

  • Import documentation & Incoterms guidance
  • Duty & tariff analysis
  • Logistics & freight coordination
  • Supplier diversification & nearshoring
