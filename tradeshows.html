🏆Canton Fair — Guangzhou, China
CF

The world's largest trade exhibition. We're there.

The China Import and Export Fair — universally known as the Canton Fair — is held twice yearly in Guangzhou, China. With over 25,000 exhibitors across three phases, it is the single most important sourcing event in the world for businesses looking to access Chinese manufacturing.

Each edition spans 15 days and three product phases: electronics and machinery; consumer goods, gifts, and home décor; and textiles, apparel, and food. Together, they cover virtually every product category relevant to US importers.

On3 Link attends the Canton Fair as a core part of our supplier development strategy. We walk the floors with our clients' product needs in mind — identifying qualified manufacturers, attending meetings, collecting samples, negotiating initial pricing, and building the face-to-face relationships that lead to reliable long-term supply.

For our clients, this means access to Canton Fair intelligence without the cost or complexity of international travel — and without the risk of navigating thousands of exhibitors without experience or language capability.

"The Canton Fair isn't just a trade show. It's where China's manufacturing capability is on full display — and where the most important supplier relationships in our network were first built."

What We Do at Canton Fair

  • Targeted manufacturer identification by product category
  • In-person supplier meetings and capability assessment
  • Product inspection and sample collection
  • Initial price and MOQ negotiation
  • Factory credibility and export experience verification
  • New product and trend identification
  • Post-show supplier shortlist and report for clients

Canton Fair Fast Facts

  • Held twice yearly — Spring (April) and Autumn (October)
  • Location: Guangzhou, Guangdong Province, China
  • Over 25,000 exhibitors per edition
  • 3 phases covering all major product categories
  • Buyers from 200+ countries attend each edition
  • One of the world's largest convention centers

Canton Fair — 3 Phases

  • Phase 1 — Electronics, machinery, vehicles, building materials
  • Phase 2 — Consumer goods, gifts, home products, décor
  • Phase 3 — Textiles, apparel, shoes, food, health products

🌐Global Sources Summit — Hong Kong
GS

Curated manufacturer access beyond Canton Fair.

Global Sources is one of Asia's most respected B2B trade media and event companies, connecting international buyers with verified Asian manufacturers. The Global Sources Summit and trade shows in Hong Kong serve as a high-quality complement to the Canton Fair — with a focus on curated, verified suppliers across electronics, fashion accessories, home products, and gifts.

While the Canton Fair offers breadth — thousands of exhibitors across every category — Global Sources events offer depth, with more rigorous supplier verification, stronger focus on export-ready manufacturers, and a buyer community that skews toward experienced importers and private label brands.

On3 Link leverages Global Sources to identify manufacturers who are specifically positioned to serve North American buyers — companies with export experience, English-language capability, and the quality systems required to meet US market standards.

"Global Sources complements Canton Fair perfectly — where Canton gives us breadth across the entire Chinese manufacturing base, Global Sources gives us depth into the manufacturers best positioned to serve North American buyers."

What We Do at Global Sources

  • Identify export-ready, verified manufacturers
  • Source suppliers with proven US/Canada export experience
  • Evaluate electronics, consumer goods, and accessories
  • Attend Summit sessions on sourcing strategy and market trends
  • Build relationships with suppliers focused on North American standards
  • Collect product samples and pricing documentation

Global Sources Overview

  • Focus on verified, export-ready manufacturers
  • Strong electronics, fashion, and home product coverage
  • Buyer community of experienced importers worldwide

🏭Factory Visits & On-Site Audits
FV

The most important step most sourcing companies skip.

Trade shows introduce us to manufacturers. Factory visits is where we truly get to know them.

A factory visit gives us something no online profile, trade show booth, or video call can provide: a firsthand look at how a manufacturer actually operates. We see the production floor, inspect quality control processes, verify equipment and capacity, meet the management team, and assess whether this is a supplier we would confidently recommend to our clients.

On3 Link conducts direct factory visits across China, Brazil, Mexico, and the United States. These visits are not optional extras — they are a core part of our supplier development methodology, and they are why our supplier recommendations carry a level of confidence that online sourcing simply cannot match.

"Walking a factory floor tells you more about a supplier in one hour than six months of email communication. It's where we separate the manufacturers we trust from the ones we walk away from."

What We Evaluate on Factory Visits

  • Production capacity and equipment quality
  • Quality control processes and inspection stations
  • Worker count and operational scale
  • Certifications displayed and documentation available
  • Cleanliness, organization, and operational standards
  • Management team experience and communication quality
  • Sample products and current production review
  • Export history and reference client documentation

Countries We Visit Factories In

  • 🇨🇳 China — Guangdong, Zhejiang, Jiangsu
  • 🇧🇷 Brazil — São Paulo, Florianópolis, Rio Grande do Sul
  • 🇲🇽 Mexico — Monterrey, Guadalajara, Bajío
  • 🇺🇸 USA — Domestic manufacturer audits

Factory Visit Deliverables

  • Written factory assessment report
  • Photo documentation of production floor
  • Capacity and capability verification
  • Quality system evaluation summary
  • Recommendation: approve / conditional / decline
  • Supplier profile for client file
