Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes On3 Link different from a freight forwarder or customs broker?▾

On3 Link is a strategic international trade partner, not a logistics provider. While freight forwarders move cargo and customs brokers handle paperwork, we work upstream—identifying the right suppliers, building relationships, negotiating terms, and helping you make better purchasing decisions. We focus on your business strategy, not just your shipment.

Which countries do you operate in?▾

Our active network spans the United States, Brazil, China, and Mexico. These four markets represent the most strategic opportunities for American businesses looking to source products, diversify supply chains, or explore nearshoring options.

Can you help with Private Label and OEM products?▾

Absolutely. Private label and OEM/ODM manufacturing is one of our core specialties. We connect you with verified factories capable of producing to your specifications, manage sampling, quality control, and production coordination from start to finish.

How does the initial consultation work?▾

Your first consultation is free. We'll discuss your business, your product needs, your current supply chain challenges, and your goals. From there, we'll put together a clear project plan with scope, timeline, and investment. No obligation, no pressure—just a strategic conversation.

Do you attend trade shows on behalf of clients?▾

Yes. Trade show representation is one of our services. We attend major international trade events including the Canton Fair and Global Sources on behalf of clients—identifying new suppliers, attending meetings, collecting samples, and building relationships that would otherwise require you to travel internationally.

What industries do you work with?▾

We serve businesses across construction, beauty and barber, consumer products, retail, wholesale, private label brands, e-commerce, automotive, home improvement, manufacturing, and more. If your business sources products internationally—or wants to—we can help.
